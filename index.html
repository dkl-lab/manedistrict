Hub 07 · For Professionals

Bring your chair to our clients.

Mane District and Braids Champs are always looking for licensed, insured stylists and beauticians across the UK. Apply below and our team will follow up to start your vetting process.

Requirements to join

  • ✓

    Valid DBS Certificate (Enhanced)Every professional must hold a valid Enhanced DBS (Disclosure and Barring Service) check. This is non-negotiable — it protects our clients and is a legal best practice for anyone working in people's homes. If you don't have one yet, you can enrol below.

  • ✓

    Professional licence or qualificationA recognised UK hair, beauty, or makeup qualification (e.g. NVQ Level 2/3, City & Guilds, VTCT, or equivalent). We review each application on its merits.

  • ✓

    Public liability insuranceYou must hold your own public liability insurance (minimum £1M cover) before your first booking. This protects both you and the client.

  • ✓

    Your own professional equipmentAs a mobile professional, you'll bring your own kit to every booking. We don't supply equipment.

  • ✓

    Right to work in the UKYou must have the legal right to work in the United Kingdom as a self-employed individual.

Don't have a DBS certificate yet? You can apply directly through the government's official service — takes around 2 weeks and costs £18 for an Enhanced check.

Who we are

Mane District ("we", "us", "our") operates a mobile hair and beauty booking platform connecting clients with vetted, independent hair and beauty professionals across the UK. This policy explains what personal data we collect, why, and what rights you have over it, in line with UK GDPR and the Data Protection Act 2018.

What we collect

Depending on how you use the site, we may collect:

  • Clients booking a service: name, email, phone number, service requested, preferred date/time, and the address where the service will take place.
  • Stylists, braiders, and makeup artists applying to join: name, email, phone number, city, specialty, experience details, and DBS (Disclosure and Barring Service) certificate information provided for vetting purposes.
  • Staff accounts: login email, used solely to access the internal operations dashboard.

We do not knowingly collect special category data (such as health information) beyond what you voluntarily include in free-text fields, and we ask that you avoid including anything sensitive unless directly relevant to a booking.

Why we collect it

  • To process and fulfil bookings between clients and professionals
  • To assess and vet applications from professionals wanting to join the platform, including DBS verification
  • To contact you about a booking or application you've made
  • To operate the optional refundable deposit system via Stripe (where used)

Our legal basis for processing this data is performance of a contract (fulfilling your booking or application) and, where applicable, our legitimate interest in vetting professionals to keep clients safe.

Who we share it with

We use the following third-party processors to operate the platform. Each only receives the data necessary for their function:

  • Supabase — our database host, storing booking and application records
  • Stripe — payment processing for deposits and memberships (we never see or store your card details directly)
  • Vercel — website hosting
  • Awin — our affiliate network for product recommendations; visiting an affiliate link may set a tracking cookie on the retailer's site, not ours (see our Cookie Policy)

We do not sell personal data to third parties.

How long we keep it

Booking records are retained for as long as needed for service delivery and basic accounting purposes. Application records, including DBS information, are retained for the duration of a professional's active status with Mane District, plus a reasonable period afterward to meet our safeguarding obligations. You can request earlier deletion at any time (see "Your rights" below), subject to any legal retention requirements.

Your rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the right to access, correct, delete, or export your personal data, and to object to or restrict certain processing. To exercise any of these rights, email us at urbanmane@protonmail.com and we'll respond within one month.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent to urbanmane@protonmail.com. If you're unhappy with how we've handled your data, you also have the right to complain to the UK Information Commissioner's Office (ico.org.uk).

What this page is

This site keeps cookie use to a minimum. Here's exactly what's in use today, and what isn't.

What we currently use

  • Essential session data only: when you log in as staff, Supabase Authentication sets a session cookie/token so you stay logged in. This is strictly necessary for the admin panel to function and isn't used for tracking.

What we don't currently use

  • No advertising or marketing cookies
  • No analytics or visitor-tracking cookies (e.g. Google Analytics) are currently installed
  • No cross-site tracking cookies of our own

Affiliate links and third-party cookies

When you click a "Shop now" link on our Products page, you leave our site and land on a retailer's website (such as LookFantastic) via our Awin affiliate tracking link. That retailer, and Awin, may set their own cookies on their own domain to track that the visit came from us — this happens on their site, not ours, and is governed by their own cookie and privacy policies, not this one.

If we add analytics or marketing cookies in the future, we'll update this page and, where required by law, ask for your consent first via a cookie banner.

Hub 08 · Help Centre

Answers to common questions.

Can't find what you need? Email us directly at urbanmane@protonmail.com.

How do I book a stylist?

Go to the Book a Stylist page, choose your service, date, time, and address, and submit the form. We'll confirm your stylist match by email.

Is payment taken upfront?

No payment is required to submit a booking request. You can optionally secure your slot with a refundable deposit, taken securely via Stripe.

Are stylists background-checked?

Yes. Every professional on Mane District and Braids Champs passes identity verification, a criminal background check, and holds valid insurance before they can accept bookings.

Which cities do you operate in?

We currently operate in London and Manchester, with Birmingham, Bristol, Edinburgh, and Leeds launching soon. See the Cities page for live updates.

How do I become a stylist on the platform?

Visit the Apply to Join page and submit your details. Our team will follow up by email to begin the vetting and verification process.

Can I cancel or reschedule?

Yes — email us at urbanmane@protonmail.com with your booking details as early as possible, and we'll help you reschedule or cancel.
