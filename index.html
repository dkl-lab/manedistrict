Hub 07 · For Professionals

Bring your chair to our clients.

Mane District and Braids Champs are always looking for licensed, insured stylists and beauticians across the UK.

Requirements to join

  • ✓

    Valid DBS Certificate (Enhanced)Every professional must hold a valid Enhanced DBS check. Non-negotiable — it protects our clients. Don't have one yet? Enrol below.

  • ✓

    Professional licence or qualificationA recognised UK hair, beauty, or makeup qualification (NVQ Level 2/3, City & Guilds, VTCT, or equivalent).

  • ✓

    Public liability insuranceMinimum £1M cover. You must hold this before your first booking.

  • ✓

    Your own professional equipmentAs a mobile professional, you bring your own kit to every booking.

  • ✓

    Right to work in the UKYou must have the legal right to work in the UK as a self-employed individual.

Don't have a DBS certificate yet? Apply directly through the government's official service — takes around 2 weeks, costs £18 for an Enhanced check.

Who we are

Mane District operates a mobile hair and beauty booking platform connecting clients with vetted, independent professionals across the UK. This policy explains what personal data we collect, why, and your rights under UK GDPR and the Data Protection Act 2018.

What we collect

  • Clients booking: name, email, phone, service, date/time, and address.
  • Professionals applying: name, email, phone, city, specialty, experience, and DBS certificate information.
  • Staff accounts: login email only.

Why we collect it

  • To process and fulfil bookings
  • To vet professionals applying to join, including DBS verification
  • To contact you about a booking or application
  • To process optional deposits via Stripe

Who we share it with

  • Stripe — payment processing

We do not sell personal data.

Hub 08 · Help Centre

Answers to common questions.

Can't find what you need? Email urbanmane@protonmail.com

How do I book a stylist?

Go to Book a Stylist, choose your service, date, time, and address, and submit. We'll confirm your stylist match by email.

Is payment taken upfront?

No payment is required to submit a booking. You can optionally secure your slot with a refundable deposit via Stripe.

Are stylists background-checked?

Yes. Every professional holds a valid Enhanced DBS certificate, personally reviewed by our team before they can accept bookings.

Which cities do you operate in?

Currently London and Manchester. Birmingham, Bristol, Edinburgh, and Leeds launching soon.

How do I become a stylist on the platform?

Visit Apply to Join and submit your details. Our team will follow up to begin vetting and DBS verification.

Can I cancel or reschedule?

Yes — email urbanmane@protonmail.com with your booking details as early as possible.
